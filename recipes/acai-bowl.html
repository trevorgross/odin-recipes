Açaí Bowl

This is a bowl of Açaí

This is probably delicious and refreshing

Ingredients

  • 1 sambazon açaí fruit bowl
  • 1 banana

  • 1 tablespoon (11 grams) flaxseed meal
  • 1 tablespoon nido dry milk powder
  • 2 tablespoon (15 grams) granola
  • 2 tablespoon (32 grams) almond butter
  • 2 ounce (5⁄12 cups) blueberry

Steps

  1. Divide the açaí between serving bowls. Rinse the fruits, and divide all toppings between bowls.
